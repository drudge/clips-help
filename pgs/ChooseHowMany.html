Choose how many clips to save at a time

If you'd like to store more or less clips at a time, the choice is yours. The more clips you store at once, the more system resources Clips will consume.

Here is how you choose:
●

Open Preferences

●

Click on the "General" tab

●

Drag the slider under "Automatically create clips from system clipboard" to your desired number of clips
