Creating a new clip

When Clips is running you won't even notice it. Its advanced architecture allows it to sit in the background and silently add anything you copy. This is called Clipboard Monitoring.

Adding clips requires no additional steps.

Clipboard Monitoring, which is enabled by default, silently monitors everything that is copied to your Mac's clipboard. This means that new clips are automatically created for everything you are working with simply by using the usual Command-C shortcut.
