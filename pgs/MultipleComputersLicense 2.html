Can I use my license on more than one computer at a time?

Absolutely. A single license can be used on up to (2) machines simultaneously. If you need to use the software on more than 2 machines at the same time, you will need to purchase more licenses (such as a 5-Pack or additional single licenses).

The alternative to purchasing additional licenses would be to deactivate the software on your machines when you aren't using it so that the license becomes available for use again.
