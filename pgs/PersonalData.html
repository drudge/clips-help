Is there any personal information transmitted during the activation process?

No. We take your privacy very seriously, and never use any personally identifiable information, neither in the creation of the serial numbers or during transmission.

You may optionally send anonymous usage statistics through Sparkle Update. Again, there is no personally identifiable information transmitted during this process.

What kind of data is transmitted?
●

Version numbers mostly. The application version and buid numbers, Mac OS X system version number, CPU speed, amount of RAM, etc.

Why is this kind of information useful?
●

Knowing what software and hardware our customers use helps us develop features that work well on the machines you guys use. It helps us determine what acceptble limits should be set for your available system resources.
