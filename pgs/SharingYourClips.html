Sharing your clips

Have multipe computers? Want to share your templates and snippets with your co-workers? Clips has you covered with clipboard sharing.

Here is how you share:
●

Open Preferences

●

Click on the "Sharing" tab

●

Check "Share my clipboard library on my local network"

●

Adjust shared clipboard name if desired

To view shared clipboards, you'll have to enable shared clipboard discovery.

Finding shared clipboards
●

Open Preferences

●

Click on the "Sharing" tab

●

Check "Look for shared clipboards"

●

You will now see any available shared clipboard libraries in the sidebars of both the Clips Organizer and Board.
